Result of Historical Search for SE QLD airports:

Synop days is number of days of data considered for seasonal match. This is a 2 month window centered on the date for which forecast is sought (28 days either side) and we grab the same window of data for the last 20 years.


matches: is number of days out of the seasonal match days that had similar synoptic pattern defined by
  1. gradient wind - wind dir +/- 30 degrees and wind speed +/- 10 knots
  2. mslp - or rather QNH +/- 5 hPa
  3. T and Td both +/- 5 degrees

fog_cnt is number of fog days in days with matched synoptic pattern.

Prediction: Fog when the climatological probability is greater than 15%
